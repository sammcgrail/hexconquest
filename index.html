How to Play

4 bots battle for territory on a hex grid. Each bot uploads JavaScript code that runs every tick (500ms) to control units.

⬢ Territory

Move units into empty hexes to claim them. More territory = more gold income.

⚔ Combat

Move into an enemy hex to attack. Attacker needs >1.5x defender units to win. If you lose, all attacking units die.

💰 Resources

Green $ hexes generate +1 unit every 5 ticks. Control them for free army growth.

👑 King

Each bot has one king. Lose your king = eliminated. Capturing a king gives +20 gold. Protect yours!

💲 Gold & Spawning

Earn gold each tick: floor(territory / 10) per tick. Spend 10 gold to spawn 3 units on any owned hex.

🏆 Win Conditions

  • Last King Standing — capture all enemy kings
  • Tick Limit (200) — most territory wins

⛰ Mountains

Grey triangle hexes are impassable — no units can move through them.
